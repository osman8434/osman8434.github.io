Classic Lasagne

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: Quick
Ingredients Quantity
Onion 1
Beef Mince 450 gr
Lasagne Sheets 4

Step 1:Preheat oven to 180

Step 2:Fry the onions in 1 tbsp of olive oil in a hot frying pan until soft

Step 3:Add in mince and break up into small bits with a spatula or spoon that you’re using to stir

Spaghetti Bolognese

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: Quick
Ingredients Quantity
Mushrooms 450 gr
Olive oil 2 lt
Spaghetti 1 packet

Step 1:Heat 2 tbsp of olive oil in a large frying pan. Add in the beef mince and break up with the spatula or spoon you are using

Step 2:Add in the onions and fry until soft. Add in the mushrooms

Step 3:Meanwhile, cook the spaghetti in lightly salted boiling water for 10 minutes
